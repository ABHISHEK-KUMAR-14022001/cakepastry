Simle Sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self raising flour

Notes on Ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar
Selt raising flour
A per mixed combination of flour leavenning agent

Method

  1. Preheat the oven at 190 degree centigrate
  2. Grease a 20 cm round cake pan
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs and caster sugar untill fluffy
  4. Flod the flour
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven or untill the top of the springs back when lightly pressed
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
